The HFJS team · Accra

Started in 2021.
Built on one idea.

A clean building and a pest-free building are the same job. Most companies in Accra do one or the other — so property managers end up juggling a cleaning contractor, a fumigator, and hoping the two don't undo each other's work. We built HFJS to do both, under one supervised team, with one standard.

Since 2021 that idea has taken us from homes and estates around Pantang and Abokobi to some of the most demanding facilities in Ghana — banking halls, hospital wards, hotels, factories and construction handovers — using trained personnel, approved chemicals and modern equipment on every job.

We work eco-friendly wherever the job allows, because the point of hygiene is health: every space we service should be left cleaner, healthier and safer than we found it — for the people in it, not just for the inspection.

On the job across Accra

What makes us different.

Not slogans — the four working habits our clients mention when they recommend us.

01

Supervised, not just staffed

Every project runs with an active supervisor on site — trained, uniformed teams working a documented routine, never left to figure it out alone.

02

Safe chemicals, handled properly

Safe, approved products, correct dilution, PPE, warning signage during fumigation, and written ventilation and re-entry guidance after it — safety is a procedure here, not a promise.

03

Equipment most cleaners don't have

Pressure washers, industrial vacuums, floor scrubbers and polishers, steamers, fogging machines and telescopic pole systems — which is why we can take on high-rise, post-construction and industrial work.

04

You sign off, or we're not done

Every job ends with a final inspection against our quality checklist, confirmed by you — and after-service support if anything needs attention later.
