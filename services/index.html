Before you book — the six questions everyone asks.

Is fumigation safe for my children and pets?
Yes — safety is procedural at HFJS. We use safe, approved chemicals, correct dilution, PPE and warning signage during treatment, and we give you written ventilation and re-entry guidance so you know exactly when it's safe to return. Children and pets simply stay out of the treated area until the stated re-entry time.
How long after fumigation before we can go back inside?
Typically 3–5 hours after a simple spraying and 12–24 hours after heavy treatment. Your exact re-entry time is confirmed in writing for your specific job.
How much do your services cost?
Every job is priced individually, because a two-bedroom apartment and a banking hall are not the same job. Message us on WhatsApp with photos and a short description of the space, and you receive a written quotation covering scope, timeline and payment terms — free, with no obligation.
Which areas do you serve?
We're based at Pantang, Accra, and work across Greater Accra daily — Adenta, Madina, Abokobi, East Legon, Spintex, Tema and beyond — with jobs across Ghana on request.
How often should I fumigate my home or office?
It depends on the pest and the environment: for most homes and food businesses in Accra, a quarterly treatment keeps infestations from re-establishing, while high-risk sites go monthly. We offer both as contracts, and we'll tell you honestly what your space actually needs.
Do you bring your own equipment and supplies?
Everything — pressure washers, industrial vacuums, floor scrubbers and polishers, steamers, fogging machines, telescopic pole systems, and approved chemicals. For high-rise work we bring boom lifts, rope access and water-fed pole systems. You provide access; we provide the rest.
